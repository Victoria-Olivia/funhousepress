Founder of Fun House Press

Hi, I’m Victoria Olivia, the founder of Fun House Press, a small creative brand focused on cute, joyful coloring books for kids and kids at heart. Fun House Press began as a way to spark creativity for my child and has grown into a collection of fun, uplifting, and imagination-filled coloring books designed for all ages.

At Fun House Press, we publish each coloring book with love and focus on choosing adorable characters, playful ideas, and comforting styles that come together to make coloring simple, joyful, and fun for all ages. Each book is thoughtfully assembled to reflect what Fun House Press is all about: creativity, positivity, and fun.

Thank you for supporting Fun House Press and helping bring more color, happiness, and imagination into the world.
